Frequently Asked Questions

What is jomo?

jomo is a dynamic customer reactivation agent and online business presence manager built specifically for small and local businesses. It helps you re-engage lapsed customers and keep your online profiles accurate and up to date.

When will jomo be available?

jomo is currently in development. We're working hard to deliver a polished experience. Check back at horoco.co for updates on availability.

What platforms will jomo support?

jomo will be available on the web, Android, and iPhone.
